Initiative

As stated on our Home Page the virus has affected a lot of people. This has especially affected a few families in our society. It was difficult choosing if we should help a lot of families or a few families right now and we realised that it would be difficult helping a lot of people right now and it would be a better idea if we could help a few families currently until we are able to get external support (sponsers etc) to expand our initiative.

Family One

This amazing family of 3 (a wife,husband and 1 child) has been heavily affected by the corona virus just as much as anyone else. However due to the circumstances the family already had, the virus made a huge impact. The two amazing breadwinners of the family do all they can for their family as they try to do as many jobs as they possibly can to make sure the family is sheltered and have food in their mouths. Family Three

This family of 4 work hard. With 2 children in school the parents do piece jobs and kids try to pitch in when they can by selling items when they can after they get back from school due to health restrictions in the country this has been difficult for them to find money and as such they have had to make more sacrifices for them to survive these disheartening times.
