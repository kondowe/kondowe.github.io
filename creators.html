On this page we will introduce you to the people behind this amazing initiative. This initiative was created by a few amazing youths of Pretoria that wanted to put other people's needs at hand before their own. The diversity in this group also speaks volumes about how they believe that we should all be working together and helping out our society. The group of creators range from young students in high school to students finishing their masters degree. We will however only discuss and introduce the main people involved in the initiative.
